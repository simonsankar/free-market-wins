## The Reigning, Defending, Undefeated Champion: The Free Market

This site is a comprehensive case against every form of coercive ethics and planned economy. The arguments converge on three irrefutable pillars:

1. **Aggression is logically indefensible** — the [[Non-Aggression Principle]] follows directly from the nature of argumentation itself
2. **Central planning is impossible, not merely inefficient** — the [[Economic Calculation Problem]] is a fatal epistemological flaw, not a management challenge
3. **Free markets win every metric that matters** — in theory and in the historical record, without exception
